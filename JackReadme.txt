Use the WASD keys to move Ian around the environment. W moves him forward, A moves him left, S moves him backward, and D moves him right.
To look around, simply move your mouse. The camera will follow your mouse movements, allowing you to explore the surroundings.

 inventory system:

You can store items in four different slots by pressing the numbers 1, 2, 3, or 4 on your keyboard. Each slot corresponds to a position in your inventory.
When you want to select an item that you have stored in a specific slot, simply press the corresponding number key. The item will be selected and ready for use.

If you wish to drop the currently selected item, press the "R" key on your keyboard. This will remove the item from your inventory and drop it in the game world.
